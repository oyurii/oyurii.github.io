Simulation Laboratory (SimLab)

This resource contains the results of computer simulation of physical processes in pure metals and their alloys under conditions of radiation exposure. The results of our research are also presented here. In our works, we use the methods of Ab-initio, Molecular dynamics, and Rate theory. To implement the simulation, we have developed a set of programs and scripts, whose operation can be controlled remotely via the web interface.
