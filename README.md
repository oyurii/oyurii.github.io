# Yurii Ovcharenko

Physicist, educator, and researcher specializing in **computational modeling of materials** and **solid state physics**.

I am a **Senior Researcher** at the *Institute of Applied Physics, NAS of Ukraine*, where I study physical processes in metal alloys for nuclear applications. I also teach **Molecular Dynamics and Ab Initio Modeling in Materials Science** to graduate students.

Previously, I was an **Associate Professor** at *Sumy State University*, teaching:
- Quantum Mechanics  
- Statistical Physics  
- Solid State Physics  

**Research interests:** molecular dynamics, *ab initio* methods, radiation-resistant materials, and computational materials science.

More detailed information about my work and contact information can be found at the link [oyurii.github.io](https://oyurii.github.io).
